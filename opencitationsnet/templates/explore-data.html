Explore the data

JISC Open Citations allows interaction with its dataset in several ways:

Journals Browse all journals referenced within the PubMed Central open-access subset. Articles Browse a selection of articles contained within or referenced by the PubMed Central open-access subset. Search Search the collection by identifier (ISBN, DOI, ISSN, etc.) or word or phrase. SPARQL query Query the dataset using SPARQL queries. Source data Download the full dataset as N-Quads or BibJSON files to manipulate off-line or load into your own system.

Open Citations is a public RDF triplestore for biomedical literature citations. Our aim is to harvest the reference lists from many current and recent open access journal articles, and to convert these datasets into RDF, starting with those in UK Pubmed Central.

The project was funded by the JISC's jiscEXPO strand.

This website is an exemplar, allowing access to roughly one tenth of the data in the PubMed Central Open Access corpus. The entire dataset can be downloaded from our source data page.
